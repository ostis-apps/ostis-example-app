Hydrogen bond - one of the types of intermolecular bonds. Mainly due to the electrostatic forces. For the occurrence of hydrogen bonding necessary that in the molecule had one or more hydrogen atoms bonded to a small but electronegative atoms, for example: O, N, F. It is important that these are electronegative atoms lone electron pair. Hydrogen bonds characteristic for substances such as water H2O, ammonia NH3, hydrogen fluoride HF.
